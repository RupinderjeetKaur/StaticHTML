Where the money goes…

We believe that transparency is extremely important and want to be clear that there are absolutely zero administration fees deducted from donations independent of banking charges.

Fees deducted by Paypal for transfers, exchanges, processing… etc. are reimbursed by an independent donor. This allows us to use the payment gateway without too much concern for it’s fees.

All administration fee’s and salaries incurred by our organization are raised through independent funding programs such as our school, workshop, farm and, occasionally, by donations specifically given by family and friends for these costs.
